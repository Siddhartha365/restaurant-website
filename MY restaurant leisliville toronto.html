About Us

**About Us** Welcome to Nola Eatery Bar, your culinary haven in Leslieville, Toronto! We specialize in an exciting fusion of Italian and French cuisine, enhanced with a unique North American twist. At Nola, we take pride in blending the rich traditions of Italian and French cooking, creating dishes that celebrate creativity and local flavors. From our hand-crafted pasta to our delectable sauces and artisanal breads, every dish is crafted with the finest ingredients to ensure an unforgettable dining experience. Our warm and inviting atmosphere is perfect for any occasion, whether you’re enjoying a romantic dinner, a lively night out with friends, or a cozy family gathering. Our dedicated team is committed to providing exceptional service, ensuring that every visit feels special. Join us at Nola Eatery Bar for a culinary journey that harmonizes the best of Italian and French flavors, all served with a touch of North American charm. We can’t wait to welcome you to the vibrant Leslieville community! Visit us at Nolaeaterybar.com for an unsurpassed dining experience in New French and Italian fusion of North American Cuisine. We serve special drinks, fresh house-made food from 20 years’ experience chef, special wine, and beer.
