About Us

Welcome to Nola Eatery & Bar, now open for our all day you will be entranced by our beautiful restaurant adorned with a fusion of French and Italian North American style food, featuring full dining filled with amazing sparkling lights, fresh flowers, and the melodic sounds of music. Voted ‘Best Romantic Restaurant,’ you will savor our "New North American Cuisine” that will captivate your palate. We serve only the finest, freshest, and organic ingredients; from our pastries to our finest meats and freshly cut seafood. If you're looking for lighter fare, visit our place where you will enjoy drink specials every day. Visit us at Nolaeaterybar.ca for an unsurpassed dining experience in New French and Italian fusion of North American Cuisine. We serve special drinks, fresh house-made food from 20 years’ experience chef, special wine, and beer.
